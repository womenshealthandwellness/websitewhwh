ABOUT US

At Women’s Health and Wellness at UTM, we create an inclusive space where women can access resources and support to prioritize their physical, mental, and emotional well-being. As a student-led organization, we foster a community that values all aspects of a woman’s health.

We bridge gaps in health education through workshops, resources, and support services that empower women to make informed choices. We believe wellness is about balance, self-care, and community.

Whether you’re seeking mental health resources, fitness tips, or reproductive health guidance, we offer a safe space where women can feel heard and supported. Through events and partnerships, we help every woman thrive.
